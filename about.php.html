About Us

Meet Darrell E. Hoard Safety Leader with a Mission

  • Expertise

  • Experience

  • Commitment

Darrell E. Hoard founded his safety consulting company with a simple belief: every worker deserves to go home safe at the end of the day. With more than 12 years of environmental, health, and safety experience across energy, renewable energy, telecom, industrial, and clinical settings, Darrell brings a practical, people-first approach to helping companies build stronger safety programs and healthier workplace cultures.

  • Workplace Safety Assessments

  • Regulatory Compliance Consulting

  • Risk Management Solutions

  • Health & Safety Management Systems

Read more

Before building his career in occupational safety, Darrell served for 12 years in the United States Navy as a Hospital Corpsman. That military experience shaped his commitment to preparedness, discipline, emergency response, and protecting people in high-pressure environments—values that continue to guide the way he works with clients today.

  • Workplace Safety Audits & Inspections

  • Risk Assessment & Hazard Management

  • OSHA & Regulatory Compliance Support

Read more

Darrell specializes in OSHA compliance, safety management systems, risk assessment, incident investigation, audits, inspections, and workforce training. Whether supporting office personnel, field crews, contractors, supervisors, or leadership teams, he focuses on clear communication, practical solutions, and measurable results that help organizations move beyond compliance and create safer, more accountable workplaces.
